Common questions

Do I need an account? No — you can browse the map, markets, vendors, and search without signing in. An account only lets you save favorites across devices.

Why does the app ask for my location? Only to show markets near you. If you decline, the map opens to a default area and everything else still works.

How do I report a vendor, market, or a problem? Use the Report or Send Feedback options inside the app, or email support@popupanalytics.app.

How do I delete my account? In the app: Settings → Delete account. Your personal info is removed.
